# Plans & Feature Matrix

Cutctx ships in four tiers. The free Builder tier includes the full
compression pipeline — the paid tiers add team analytics, org management,
and enterprise governance. Entitlements are enforced at runtime: admin
surfaces and tier-gated features perform fail-closed checks against the
active tier, and a configured license key is validated at startup (its plan
overrides any locally declared tier; expired or invalid licenses fail closed
to Builder).

## Tier overview

| Tier | Annual | Target |
|---|---|---|
| **Builder** | $0 | Individual engineers, OSS evaluators |
| **Team** | $18,000 | Single engineering team |
| **Business** | $42,000 | Platform teams, multi-project orgs |
| **Enterprise** | $60,000–$150,000+ | Security-sensitive, procurement-heavy orgs |

Monthly billing is available at a 20% premium over annual.

## How enforcement works

- The proxy resolves its tier from a validated license when `license_key`
  is configured; the validated plan always wins over a locally declared
  tier, and expired/invalid licenses fail closed to Builder.
- Tier-gated features refuse to activate on unentitled deployments — at
  startup, and on runtime config toggles — with an explicit
  `feature_not_available` error naming the required tier.
- Reading status or savings data never requires a paid tier.
