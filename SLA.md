# CutCtx Support Policy and SLA

This document captures the customer-facing support expectations for CutCtx. It is the published version of the support policy referenced during commercial onboarding and procurement.

## Coverage By Tier

| Tier | Support Channel | Coverage | Target Response |
|------|-----------------|----------|-----------------|
| Builder | Community | Best effort | No SLA |
| Team | Email | Business hours | Next business day |
| Business | Email plus scheduled calls | Business hours | 4 business hours |
| Enterprise | Priority channel plus escalation | 24/7 for critical issues | 1 hour for critical issues |

## Severity Levels

| Severity | Definition |
|----------|------------|
| Critical | Production outage or security-impacting blocker |
| High | Core feature unavailable with no reasonable workaround |
| Medium | Degraded behavior with workaround available |
| Low | Documentation, usability, or advisory issue |

## What Support Includes

- installation and deployment guidance
- configuration assistance
- issue triage
- upgrade planning
- governance feature enablement for paid tiers

## What Support Does Not Include By Default

- custom product development
- unlimited architecture consulting
- customer-managed upstream provider incidents
- third-party cluster administration beyond agreed scope

## Enterprise Escalation Path

1. Initial triage
2. Engineering escalation
3. Leadership escalation for unresolved critical issues

## Commercial Note

Formal support credits, service levels, and remedies may be superseded by an executed order form or MSA addendum. This file is the public policy baseline used by the team until a custom contract overrides it.
