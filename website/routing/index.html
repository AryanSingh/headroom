A safe decision path

The route has to prove itself.

CutCtx does not treat a short prompt as automatically safe to downgrade. The request, available target, and transport must all satisfy the policy before a route can apply.

01 Request

Understand the work

Complexity, recent tool context, code, security, and other risk signals can keep the requested model in place.

02 Capabilities

Match the contract

Tool calling, structured output, vision, audio, streaming, and other required capabilities must be supported by the target.

03 Transport

Prove the path

Provider, account, and transport proof constrain which targets can be considered.

04 Readiness

Require eligibility

Readiness, certification, availability, and cost gates keep unknown or unsuitable candidates out.

05 Outcome

Route or retain

A route applies only when every gate passes. Otherwise, the requested model retained outcome is the correct result.
