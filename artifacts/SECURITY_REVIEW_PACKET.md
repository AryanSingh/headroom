## Review Checklist

- customer understands the self-hosted model
- customer understands what data is stored locally
- customer understands what telemetry is optional
- customer understands available admin controls
- customer understands any non-completed certification claims are out of scope

## Internal Rule

Do not imply completed external certifications or third-party audit outcomes unless they exist and are approved for disclosure.
