# Headroom Legal and Procurement Checklist

## Purpose

Track the non-engineering work required to make the commercial product contract-ready.

## Important Note

This file is an operating checklist, not legal advice. Final contract language should be approved by qualified counsel.

## Required Documents

- Master Services Agreement
- Data Processing Addendum
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Support Policy and SLA
- Security incident notification policy

## Procurement Packet

- company legal name and address
- pricing sheet
- packaging matrix
- security one-pager
- deployment architecture summary
- support and escalation policy

## Questions Counsel Should Resolve

1. What data categories does Headroom process?
2. What data categories does Headroom store locally?
3. What service commitments are being made for support and uptime?
4. What indemnity boundaries are acceptable?
5. Are there customer-hosted versus vendor-hosted distinctions in the contract language?
6. What audit and incident notification language is acceptable?

## Internal Approval Steps

### Commercial
- approve list pricing
- approve discount guardrails
- approve pilot terms

### Legal
- approve contracting templates
- approve privacy statements
- approve DPA language

### Support
- approve SLA language
- approve escalation path

## Exit Criteria

- legal templates exist
- procurement packet can be sent in one bundle
- founders know what is negotiable and what is not
