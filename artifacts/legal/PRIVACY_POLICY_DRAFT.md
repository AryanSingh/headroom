# Headroom Privacy Policy Draft

## Important Note

This draft is for internal preparation and must be reviewed by counsel before publication.

## Overview

This privacy policy should describe only the data that Headroom Labs actually collects in the commercial model being offered.

## Categories To Evaluate

- website analytics
- inbound sales and support contact information
- billing contacts
- optional aggregate product telemetry when a license key is configured

## Categories To Avoid Overstating

- prompt content
- customer code
- tool results
- centrally hosted conversation data

## Disclosure Guidance

Be explicit about:
- self-hosted versus vendor-hosted distinctions
- optional telemetry behavior
- support communications handling
- retention of sales and billing records
