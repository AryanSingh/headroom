# Headroom Terms of Service Draft

## Important Note

This draft is for internal preparation and must be reviewed by counsel before use.

## Structure

Suggested sections:
- acceptance of terms
- scope of software and services
- subscription and fees
- acceptable use
- intellectual property
- warranty disclaimer
- limitation of liability
- termination

## Drafting Guidance

- separate OSS license terms from paid subscription terms
- describe support using the support policy, not ad hoc promises
- avoid implying hosted custody of customer prompts unless that model exists
